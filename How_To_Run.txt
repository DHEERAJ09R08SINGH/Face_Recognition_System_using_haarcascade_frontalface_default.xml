### **Workflow:**

#### **Phase 1: Collect Data (15 minutes)**
```
1. Choose option 1 (Collect face data)
2. Enter your name (e.g., "John")
3. Collect 150 images
   - Face the camera
   - Move your head (left, right, up, down)
   - Change expressions (smile, neutral, serious)
   - Wait for "Captured: 150/150"

4. Repeat for another person (optional)
   - Choose option 1 again
   - Enter different name (e.g., "Sarah")
   - Collect 150 images
```

#### **Phase 2: Train Model (2-5 minutes)**
```
1. Choose option 2 (Train the model)
2. Wait for training to complete
3. Note the accuracy (should be 85%+)
```

#### **Phase 3: Test Recognition (Real-time!)**
```
1. Choose option 3 (Test real-time recognition)
2. Face the camera
3. See your name appear above your face!
4. Press 'q' to quit
```
